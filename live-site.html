About Us

About Us

Complete Instrumentation Solutions Private Limited (CISPL) was incorporated in year 2011 with its corporate offices in Gurgaon, India and now has presence in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Pune, Kolkata and Lucknow. It is private limited company managed and run by IIT alumni professionals. Our Technical Director, Mr. Neeraj Chadha, holds M.Tech (Applied Geophysics) from IIT, Roorkee and has around 34 years of experience in the field of Instrumentation. During these 34 years, he has served various capacities and has been responsible for introducing several innovative technologies in India in various domains. Some of these domains and technologies are:

Earthquake Research - Broad Band Seismometers as per GSN standards (1996), Remote Reference Magneto Tellurics (RRMT 1998) and Superconducting Gravimeters (2006)
Geodynamic Studies - Borehole Strainmeters (2007)
Geophysical Exploration- Borehole Radar for mineral exploration (1989), Digital Instantaneous Floating-Point technology in exploration seismographs (1992), MT and Controlled Source MT (CSAMT) for Oil Exploration (1993), Marine Magneto Tellurics (2005) for offshore exploration,Ocean Bottom Seismometers (2007) for gas hydrate studies
Pavement Testing - Setting up of first testing lab as per SHRP in 2001, Accelerated Load Testing Facility (2009)
Materials Testing – Accelerated Stress Rupture Testing Machine with six loading units in single frame (2014), Polyaxial Testing System for Rocks (2014), Advanced testing system for simulation and testing of gas hydrates (2020) in the field of materials testingStructural Testing –Parallel Seismic and Ultra Seismic for NDT of bridges (2007), Automated bridge load rating system (2008), Fully automated Bridge Health Monitoring System (2010)

To support local talent and entrepreneurship under Make in India initiative, he has been instrumental in developing Vehicle Mounted Intelligent Falling Weight Deflectometer and Mobile Bridge Inspection Units in India and is presently working with some more technologies under this initiative.Our philosophy is ‘Concept to Completion.’ We hand hold our prospects and client through complete journey from conceptualization to completion to after sales support and further guiding and helping them in transitioning to newer relevant technologies for better efficiency, productivity, cost optimisation and better outcomes.

We are associated with the leading companies in the said fields and offer a comprehensive suite of equipment and services that can take a project from concept to completion.

Our Geophysical Exploration Services group offers wide range of services for Oil and Gas, Coal Bed Methane, Gas Hydrates, Shale, Mineral & Water Exploration,Engineering Geophysics.

Structural Health Evaluation group offers comprehensive solutions for structural monitoring and safety. This includes the design of the system, its delivery and installation, maintenance and operation, web access to data and data analysis by experienced engineering partners. We also conduct Advanced Non Destructive Evaluation of Structures using the latest equipment/technologies.

Pavement Evaluation Services group of our company is equipped with the latest technologies to conduct functional and structural evaluation of the pavements and much more. We also conduct Independent Audits for Quantity Assurance and Quality Control.

Our Railways Testing Services group in association with overseas principals offers comprehensive services for testing of Rail Road Track, Bridges and Structures for sub surface flaws with advanced testing equipment and technologies with goal to maximize flaw detection, productivity and safety.
