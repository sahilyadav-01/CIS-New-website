About Complete Instrumentation Solutions

Engineering instrumentation and testing services from concept to completion.

Complete Instrumentation Solutions Private Limited provides high-quality materials testing, geophysical exploration, structural health monitoring, and research services to clients across India and beyond.

Company Profile

Delivering trusted engineering solutions for infrastructure and research.

Complete Instrumentation Solutions Private Limited (CISPL) was incorporated in year 2011 with its corporate offices in Gurgaon, India and now has presence in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Pune, Kolkata and Lucknow. It is private limited company managed and run by IIT alumni professionals. Our Technical Director, Mr. Neeraj Chadha, holds M.Tech (Applied Geophysics) from IIT, Roorkee and has around 34 years of experience in the field of Instrumentation. During these 34 years, he has served various capacities and has been responsible for introducing several innovative technologies in India in various domains. Earthquake Research — Broad Band Seismometers as per GSN standards (1996), Remote Reference Magneto Tellurics (RRMT 1998) and Superconducting Gravimeters (2006).
Geodynamic Studies — Borehole Strainmeters (2007).
Geophysical Exploration — Borehole Radar for mineral exploration (1989), Digital Instantaneous Floating-Point technology in exploration seismographs (1992), MT and Controlled Source MT (CSAMT) for Oil Exploration (1993), Marine Magneto Tellurics (2005) for offshore exploration, Ocean Bottom Seismometers (2007) for gas hydrate studies.
Pavement Testing — Setting up of first testing lab as per SHRP in 2001, Accelerated Load Testing Facility (2009).
Materials Testing — Accelerated Stress Rupture Testing Machine with six loading units in single frame (2014), Polyaxial Testing System for Rocks (2014), Advanced testing system for simulation and testing of gas hydrates (2020).
Structural Testing — Parallel Seismic and Ultra Seismic for NDT of bridges (2007), Automated bridge load rating system (2008), Fully automated Bridge Health Monitoring System (2010).

To support local talent and entrepreneurship under Make in India initiative, he has been instrumental in developing Vehicle Mounted Intelligent Falling Weight Deflectometer and Mobile Bridge Inspection Units in India and is presently working with some more technologies under this initiative. Our philosophy is ‘Concept to Completion.’ We hand hold our prospects and client through complete journey from conceptualization to completion to after sales support and further guiding and helping them in transitioning to newer relevant technologies for better efficiency, productivity, cost optimisation and better outcomes.
