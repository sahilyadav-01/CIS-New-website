About Complete Instrumentation Solutions

Engineering instrumentation and testing services from concept to completion.

Complete Instrumentation Solutions Private Limited provides high-quality materials testing, geophysical exploration, structural health monitoring, and research services to clients across India and beyond.

Company Profile

Delivering trusted engineering solutions for infrastructure and research.

We support government agencies, institutions, and private industry with instrumentation systems, test laboratories, survey services, and technical advisory support.

Since our founding, CIS has built a reputation for timely delivery, technical accuracy, and customized instrumentation solutions. Our teams combine geophysical expertise, pavement engineering, and structural diagnostics to serve projects in transport, energy, civil infrastructure, and environmental science.

Our portfolio includes materials testing, geodynamic studies, earthquake research, microzonation, railways, automotive & aerospace testing, and green energy instrumentation.
